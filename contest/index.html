Get to Know Us

We are passionate about technology and innovation, especially when it comes to remote work. Over the years, we have faced many challenges while working as digital nomads, particularly internet connection issues. That's why we created ConTest.

ConTest was developed to avoid these inconveniences and ensure a hassle-free work experience. ConTest is able to assess the quality of connection of our hosts, so that we can be sure that we will have a reliable and stable connection during our work sessions. With ConTest, we have become even more efficient and productive in our remote work.
